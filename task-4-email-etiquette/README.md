# Task 4: Email and Digital Etiquette

This folder contains the work completed for Task 4 of the Digital Literacy project.

# Contents

## Professional Emails
Two formal emails were drafted:
- Request for assignment submission extension (student to professor)
- Application for a summer internship opportunity

These emails demonstrate proper email structure, including subject line, greeting, clear body, and professional closing.

## Social Media Checklist
A checklist of Do’s and Don’ts for responsible social media usage is included. It highlights best practices for maintaining professionalism, protecting privacy, and ensuring safe online behavior.

## Learning Outcome
This task helped in understanding the importance of professional communication and responsible digital behavior in academic and professional environments.
